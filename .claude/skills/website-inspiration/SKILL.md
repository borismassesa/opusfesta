# Wedding Inspiration Content

## Content Types

- **Blog Posts** — wedding advice, trends, real wedding features
- **Planning Guides** — step-by-step checklists, timelines, budget guides
- **Inspiration Galleries** — curated photo collections by theme, color, style
- **Wedding Tips** — quick advice snippets and pro tips
- **Vendor Spotlights** — featured vendor stories and portfolios

## Tanzania & East Africa Context

- Reflect East African wedding traditions: Swahili customs, ngoma, kitenge fashion
- Modern fusion styles: blending traditional and contemporary aesthetics
- Budget references always in TZS (e.g., "TZS 5,000,000 - 15,000,000 for a mid-range wedding")
- Venue recommendations for Dar es Salaam, Zanzibar, Arusha, and surrounding areas
- Seasonal considerations: dry season (June-October) is peak wedding season
- Destination weddings: Zanzibar beach weddings, safari-themed celebrations
- Multi-day celebrations: mehndi, nikah, reception across multiple days
- Vendor recommendations relevant to the Tanzania market

## Tone & Style

- Warm, professional, and aspirational
- Inclusive of diverse cultural traditions (Christian, Muslim, Hindu, traditional)
- Practical and actionable advice
- Celebrate local artisans, designers, and vendors
