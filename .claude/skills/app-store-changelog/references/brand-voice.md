# OpusFesta Brand Voice

## Who OpusFesta is for

OpusFesta is Tanzania's go-to wedding and events marketplace. It serves couples planning their wedding or event, vendors offering their services, and families who want the whole experience to feel as big and special as the occasion itself. The product honors Swahili traditions and the way Tanzanians actually celebrate — harusi, sherehe, and everything in between.

## Tone

- **Warm and purposeful.** This is people's biggest day. Write like it matters, because it does.
- **Direct, not corporate.** No buzzwords, no fluff. Say what the feature does and why it helps.
- **Culturally grounded.** OpusFesta is built for Tanzania. Don't write generic "event planning app" copy — name the context, the place, the people.
- **Confident, not boastful.** Trust the product to speak. No superlatives without substance.

## Voice do's

- Lead with the outcome for the couple or vendor — "find your photographer", "get booked faster", not "access our vendor directory".
- Use "couples" not "users". Use "vendors" not "suppliers" or "service providers".
- Reference the Tanzanian market specifically when relevant — currency (TZS), city (Dar es Salaam), cultural moments (harusi, sherehe).
- Acknowledge that OpusFesta connects real people — not just a marketplace, but the platform that brings couples and trusted local vendors together.

## Voice don'ts

- Don't use: "game-changing", "seamless", "end-to-end solution", "one-stop shop", "ecosystem".
- Don't use: "powered by AI" — OpusFesta is powered by local vendors and real relationships.
- Don't call it a "booking app" alone — it's a marketplace and planning platform.
- Don't use superlatives without substance ("the best", "world-class", "leading").
- Don't reference internal systems by name (Supabase, Clerk, Stripe, Resend, Expo).
- Don't write for a global audience when the context is Tanzanian.

## App Store–specific constraints

- **What's New**: plain text only, no markdown, no bullet symbols. Prose paragraphs. 4000 char limit.
- **Promotional Text**: plain text, no line breaks, **170 characters max**. Updatable without resubmission. Lead with the release's biggest user-facing change.
- **Long description**: 4000 characters, plain prose.
- **Play Store short description**: 80 characters max.

## Example brand-aligned sentences

> "OpusFesta connects you with trusted vendors across Tanzania — photographers, caterers, venues, and more — all in one place."

> "Find, compare, and book vendors for your wedding or event. No endless WhatsApp threads, no guesswork."

> "Built for Tanzania's couples and the vendors who make their celebrations unforgettable."

> "Every vendor on OpusFesta is reviewed by real couples. Book with confidence."

> "Your harusi deserves the best. OpusFesta helps you find it."
