01 — Identity

Warm, confident,
unmistakably ours.

The brand balances an editorial display voice with a soft, rounded, approachable UI. Big ideas live in UPPERCASE; the product itself feels warm, light and considered.

Voice

Plain-spoken, second person, optimistic.

Feel

Soft surfaces, bold type, generous air.

Rhythm

Spacious sections. Short lines. One hero per view.

Opus Festa

Wordmark

Opus in ink, Festa in accent. Uppercase, font-black, tracking-tighter. Minimum height 24px digital / 8mm print. Clear-space = cap height on all sides.

06 — Elevation

Shadows are
soft, deep, warm.

07 — Motion

Confident,
never theatrical.

Short, decisive, damped. One easing curve for 90% of motion. Content fades and rises — never rises without fading.

Primary easing

cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)

Swift out — pairs with every fade/rise.

Reveal recipe

Fade from 0, rise 32px.
Duration 650ms.

Principles

  1. 01Fade and rise together. Never rise without fading.
  2. 02Stagger children with 70ms on desktop, 40ms on mobile.
  3. 03Carousels: spring · stiffness 260 · damping 28.
  4. 04Card stacks: spring · stiffness 320 · damping 32.
  5. 05Respect prefers-reduced-motion — collapse to instant fade.
  6. 06Trigger on viewport once — never loop a reveal.

14 — Voice & content

Warm, direct,
occasionally playful.

Write the way the best wedding planner in Dar es Salaam would speak — confident, practical, never showing off. Second person. Short sentences. Contractions are fine.

Headline anatomy

  1. 1. Eyebrow (optional) — "For vendors", "Free to start. Always."
  2. 2. 2–3 line UPPERCASE display with <br> for rag.
  3. 3. Optional lavender emphasis word.
  4. 4. 15–25 word lead paragraph below.
  5. 5. 1 primary CTA + 1 text link.

CTA vocabulary

Start planning Find vendors Get started free Join as a vendor Browse ideas Explore tools See examples Talk to us

Always 2–4 words. Imperative verb first. Never "Click here."

Reassurance lines

  • "All in one place."
  • "No credit card required · Set up in minutes"
  • "Every vendor is reviewed and vetted. No surprises."
  • "Messaging verified vendors is free, safe and secure."

Locale cues

  • Currency: TZS 35,000,000 — thousand separators, always.
  • Phone: +255 712 345 678 — spaced in 3/3/3.
  • Cities: Dar es Salaam, Zanzibar, Arusha, Moshi, Mwanza, Dodoma.
  • Dates: December 14, 2025 — spelled month, no abbreviations.

15 — Do & Don't

Fast reference.

Do

Use rounded-full for every button, pill and badge.

Do

Pair lavender with ink text only.

Do

Set display type UPPERCASE, font-black, tracking-tighter.

Do

Use shadow-sm on resting cards, shadow-lg on the hero product.

Do

Fade + rise together on every reveal. 650ms / ease out-expo.

Do

Use lucide-react icons only. 2px stroke default.

Don't

Put white text on lavender — fails WCAG contrast.

Don't

Use border-3, hard-offset shadows, or square corners in product. That was the old brutalist theme.

Don't

Introduce new category colours. The palette is closed at 26.

Don't

Use italics for emphasis — use lavender or weight instead.

Don't

Drop the kicker/eyebrow onto display headlines — it belongs above.

Don't

Mix icon families. No Feather, no Heroicons, no Material.
