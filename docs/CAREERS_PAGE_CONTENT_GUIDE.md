# Careers Page Content Guide

## Overview
This guide provides authentic, TheFesta-specific content recommendations for the careers page. All content should reflect that TheFesta is **Tanzania's go-to wedding & events marketplace** that connects couples with vendors and streamlines event planning.

## Hero Section

### Current (Generic)
- Title: "Careers at TheFesta"
- Description: "If every person and business can tailor event planning to their needs, the world will be better at celebrating life's moments..."

### Recommended (Authentic)
- **Title**: "Build the future of Tanzanian celebrations"
- **Description**: "We're building Tanzania's go-to wedding & events marketplace. Join us in connecting couples with trusted vendors, streamlining event planning, and making celebrations more accessible—all while honoring Swahili traditions."

## Values Section ("How we work")

### Recommended Values

1. **Celebrate Tanzanian culture**
   - "We honor Swahili traditions while building modern tools. Every feature we ship respects the way Tanzanians actually plan celebrations, from harusi to sherehe."

2. **Build for real people**
   - "We talk to couples, vendors, and families every week. Their stories shape our product. We're not building in a vacuum—we're solving real problems for real celebrations."

3. **Move with purpose**
   - "Event planning can't wait. When a couple needs a vendor or a family needs to track RSVPs, speed matters. We ship fast, but we ship right—because celebrations deserve our best."

## Benefits Section ("The upside")

### Recommended Benefits (Tanzania-specific)

1. **Medical, dental & vision**
   - "We offer competitive medical, dental, and vision insurance for employees and dependents across Tanzania."

2. **Flexible time off**
   - "We want you to take time off to rest and celebrate. TheFesta offers flexible paid vacation as well as Tanzanian public holidays."

3. **Mental health & wellbeing**
   - "You and your dependents will have access to providers that create personalized treatment plans, including therapy and EAP services."

4. **Parental leave**
   - "We offer biological, adoptive, and foster parents paid time off to spend quality time with family."

5. **Learning & development**
   - "Budget for books, courses, and conferences. We invest in your growth because your growth is our growth."

6. **Remote-first (Tanzania)**
   - "Work from anywhere in Tanzania. We have offices in Dar es Salaam, but most of our team works remotely across the country."

## Hiring Process

### Recommended Steps

1. **Application**
   - "Submit your application through our portal. Tell us about your experience building products that matter to real people."

2. **Initial Review**
   - "Our team reviews your application and portfolio. We're looking for people who understand the Tanzanian market and care about making celebrations accessible."

3. **Interview**
   - "A conversation about your experience, our mission, and how you'd contribute to building Tanzania's go-to wedding & events marketplace."

4. **Team Meeting**
   - "Meet the wider team to discuss collaboration, our values, and how we work together to serve couples and vendors across Tanzania."

5. **Offer**
   - "We extend a competitive offer and welcome you to help us transform how Tanzanians plan and celebrate their most important moments."

## Key Messaging Points

### What Makes TheFesta Different

1. **Tanzania-First**
   - We're built for Tanzanians, by Tanzanians
   - We understand local traditions (harusi, sherehe, etc.)
   - We support mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, etc.)

2. **Real Impact**
   - Every feature helps real couples plan real celebrations
   - We connect local vendors with families
   - We make event planning accessible to more people

3. **Cultural Respect**
   - We honor Swahili traditions
   - We support both Swahili and English
   - We understand Tanzanian celebration culture

4. **Technology + Tradition**
   - Modern digital tools
   - Traditional celebration values
   - Best of both worlds

## Tone & Voice

- **Authentic**: Real stories, real impact
- **Purpose-driven**: Every sentence should connect to our mission
- **Tanzanian context**: Reference local culture, traditions, and market
