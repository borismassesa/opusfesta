<DosAndDonts>
  <Do>Use shadow-sm on resting cards. Lift to shadow-md on hover.</Do>
  <Dont>Stack borders and shadows both doing the same job — pick one or layer intentionally.</Dont>
  <Do>Nest from rounded-2xl outward. Inner elements should never have larger radius than the outer card.</Do>
  <Dont>Use rounded-lg on a card that sits inside a rounded-3xl container.</Dont>
</DosAndDonts>
